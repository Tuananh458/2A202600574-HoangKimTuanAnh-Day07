# Credit Transfer Guideline (English)

## Purpose

This guideline defines the official process and review criteria for VinUniversity students who wish to transfer academic credits earned outside the university into their VinUni program.

## Eligibility

Only credits from accredited institutions are considered for transfer. The external course must be comparable in content, level, and learning outcomes to a VinUni course. Courses graded below the equivalent of a C, or taken more than five years ago, are generally not eligible.

## Application Process

1. Submit a credit transfer request to the Registrar with official transcripts and course syllabi.
2. The relevant academic department reviews the course equivalence.
3. The Registrar records approved credits, which count toward graduation requirements but do not affect the VinUni GPA.

## Limits

A maximum of 50 percent of the total program credits may be transferred from external institutions. Students should apply early, ideally before the start of their first enrolled semester, to avoid scheduling conflicts.
